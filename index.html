Financial Aid:

The importance of financial aid is becoming more apparent than ever. Increasing cost of higher education is discouraging many students from pursuing higher education, whether vocational or trade training or attending college. Many without financial aid are forced to drop out or avoid higher education in general. However, scholarships provide a valuable source of financial aid, meaning that students don’t have to be saddled by debt, and can achieve their version of academic success. Students often aren’t aware that they have so many scholarships available to them: local scholarships offered by community organizations to internationally recognized awards. We introduce students to those scholarships! Everyone should be able to achieve their academic goals, and our work guarantees that higher education is more accessible and affordable for all.

Who Are We?

We are c/2dLt Mia Canales and c/TSgt Bowie Murthy and we are a part of ca-935th AFJROTC. As Education Officer and NCO, we provide academic opportunities to the cadets in our unit.
